Found my long-lost Simpsonizeme Photo

September 22, 2013

Back in 2007 when The Simpsons Movie was released, there was also an interactive promotional site called Simpsonizeme.com which converted portrait photos into Simpsons-style cartoon avatars. The site itself is now long gone, even though people would still like to use it. So I was excited to find my own Simpsons avatar, left, while searching for something else in my digital archives. Of course, I wasted no time in making it my profile photo on Github and macaronics.com.
